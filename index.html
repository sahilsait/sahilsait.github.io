I moved halfway across the world to bet on the future
I grew up in Kerala, India and came to the Bay Area because I believe we're living through the most transformative moment in human history — the arrival of AGI. I studied CS not to watch it happen but to be part of it. The money matters too, I won't pretend otherwise. But what really drives me is the conviction that what's being created right now will reshape everything.
I build things to understand them
My first job out of college was at Amadeus — observability infrastructure, distributed systems, the kind of work that teaches you how things break at scale. A year later I interned at Amazon, building features for the accounting automation team. Now I'm wrapping up my MS at SJSU, writing my thesis on how to route queries to the right model at the right cost. I vibe-code. I try every new tool the week it drops. This is the fork in the road. I know which side I'm on.
